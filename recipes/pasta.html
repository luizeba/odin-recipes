Penne Pasta with Blue Cheese

Description

A great pasta dish that uses blue cheese!

Ingredients

  • 400g Penne Pasta
  • 25g Butter
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Garlic Clove
  • 1 tbsp Sage
  • 100g Blue Cheese
  • Toasted Walnuts

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta according to pack instructions.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter, then gently fry the onion until golden. Add the garlic and sage, fry for a further 2 mins, then remove the pan from the heat.
  3. Drain the pasta and reserve some cooking water. Stir through the buttery onions, stilton and 2 tbsp cooking water, then sprinkle with the toasted walnuts to serve.
